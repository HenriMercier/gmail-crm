Custom links




Insert pipe ('|') separated values like this: "label_value|href_value" where href_value can contain the %%EMAIL%% parameter that shall be replaced by the actual email of the sender.
Example: "All conversations|mail.google.com/mail/u/0/#search/%%EMAIL%%"

Label will be displayed next to the email of the sender and will link to https://href after replacing %%EMAIL%% by the actual email of the sender and adding https:// at the beggining of the url.
